# Overarching Theme VII: The Corrupted Beasts

**Core Concept:** The descent of the Watchers and the ravenous hunger of the Nephilim did not just affect humanity; it twisted the natural world. This theme focuses on hunting down the apex predators of the antediluvian world that have been mutated by celestial magic or driven mad by the dying ecosystem.

## Story Arc A: The Rabid Packs
*Location Span:* Plains of Nod -> Nephilim Wastes
1. **The Feral Hounds:** Slay 20 starving wild dogs that have begun hunting Sethite shepherds in Nod.
2. **The Pack Leader:** Track and defeat the massive, two-headed alpha hound leading the packs.
3. **The Poisoned Meat:** Discover the source of their madness: rotting meat left behind by Nephilim hunters.
4. **The Carrion Birds:** Clear the skies of giant, mutated vultures that are spreading plague across the plains.
5. **The Plagued Nests:** Burn the vultures' nests located high on the cliffs bordering the Wastes.
6. **The Infected Boars:** Hunt down 15 Razor-Boars that have been infected by the cursed water supply.
7. **The Boar's Tusk:** Retrieve an uninfected tusk to prove to the elders the disease is spreading.
8. **The Healing Salve:** Brew a strong natural salve to treat the nomadic herds that were bitten by the boars.
9. **The Crazed Elephants:** Calm or defeat a herd of primeval elephants that are rampaging through a Cainite outpost.
10. **The Stampede:** Prevent the panicked elephants from destroying a crucial Sethite oasis.

## Story Arc B: The Watcher-Mutations
*Location Span:* City of Enoch -> Hermon Foothills
11. **The Sorcerer's Pets:** Infiltrate the slums of Enoch and destroy the cages where Syndicate Sorcerers are experimenting on wolves.
12. **The Chimera Escapes:** A mutated fusion of lion and goat has escaped the laboratories. Hunt it down in the city streets.
13. **The Blood of the Chimera:** Return a sample of the beast's blood to a dissident alchemist for study.
14. **The Toxic Spiders:** Clear out a cavern in the Hermon foothills infested with giant arachnids feeding on Watcher-magic.
15. **The Web of Samyaza:** Burn the massive, glowing webs that are ensnaring travelers on the mountain pass.
16. **The Spider Queen:** Defeat the massive Broodmother spider deep within the cavern.
17. **The Glowing Flora:** Harvest the mutated, bioluminescent fungi the spiders were eating.
18. **The Iron-Scaled Serpents:** Hunt serpents near the city forges that have naturally developed metallic scales from the slag.
19. **The Serpent's Rattle:** Use the metallic rattles to forge an alarm system for the resistance camp.
20. **The Beast of the Forges:** Defeat a massive, ash-breathing salamander that has taken up residence in an abandoned furnace.

## Story Arc C: The Apex Predators
*Location Span:* Nephilim Wastes -> Abyssal Basins
21. **The Desert Stalker:** Track a massive, camouflaged feline predator hunting refugees in the Wastes.
22. **The Stalker's Den:** Find the predator's den and rescue the captured (but still living) refugees.
23. **The Armored Rhinoceros:** Slay a prehistoric Elasmotherium (giant rhino) that has been driven into a frenzy by giant hunters.
24. **The Broken Horn:** Recover the shattered horn of the rhino to prevent Nephilim shamans from using it in rituals.
25. **The Swamp Behemoth:** Journey to the southern basins and track an ancient, moss-covered Behemoth.
26. **The Behemoth's Parasites:** Cleanse the Behemoth by slaying the giant, magical leeches draining its life.
27. **The Abyssal Crocodiles:** Cull the population of massive crocodiles congregating near the tectonic fissures.
28. **The Crocodile King:** Defeat the ancient, scarred alpha crocodile known as 'Sobek's Shadow'.
29. **The Cave Bear:** Hunt a colossal cave bear in the mountains that has been feeding on Watcher cultists.
30. **The Bear's Pelt:** Use the magically-infused pelt to craft a cloak that grants resistance to celestial magic.

## Story Arc D: The Nephilim's Mounts
*Location Span:* Nephilim Wastes
31. **The Taming Pits:** Infiltrate a Nephilim camp where they are brutally breaking prehistoric beasts.
32. **The Broken Chains:** Sabotage the massive iron chains used to hold the beasts.
33. **The Beast-Breaker:** Assassinate the giant Overseer responsible for torturing the animals.
34. **The Freeing of the Dire-Wolves:** Release a pack of captive Dire-Wolves and guide them out of the camp.
35. **The Captured Mammoth:** Defend a massive, chained mammoth while a Sethite smith picks the lock on its collar.
36. **The Mammoth's Rampage:** Ride the freed mammoth as it destroys the giant's encampment.
37. **The Flying Terrors:** Sabotage the launch platforms where giants are attempting to mount Giant Eagles.
38. **The Eagle's Egg:** Rescue a stolen Giant Eagle egg from the warlord's tent.
39. **The Return to the Nest:** Climb a treacherous peak to return the egg to its mother.
40. **The Eagle's Blessing:** Receive a rare, passive speed buff as thanks from the Giant Eagles.

## Story Arc E: The Final Harvest (For the Ark)
*Location Span:* Global -> Abyssal Basins
41. **The Pure Bloodline:** Use a Sethite divining rod to locate animals whose bloodlines remain entirely untouched by Watcher magic.
42. **The Golden Stag:** Track and capture (without harming) a legendary Golden Stag in the Edenic Basin.
43. **The White Dove:** Rescue a flock of pristine white doves from a Cainite fowler in Enoch.
44. **The Desert Tortoise:** Safely transport an ancient, slow-moving tortoise across the Nephilim Wastes.
45. **The Mountain Goats:** Herd a flock of nimble mountain goats down from the collapsing slopes of Hermon.
46. **The Singing Birds:** Capture a pair of every species of songbird in the plains before the smog kills them.
47. **The Loading Ramp:** Escort the gathered animals up the Ark's ramp while defending them from panicked predators.
48. **The Hibernation Spell:** Assist Noah's wife in brewing a natural sedative to keep the carnivores asleep during the voyage.
49. **The Final Count:** Check the manifest to ensure exactly two of every unclean, and seven of every clean beast are aboard.
50. **The Roar of the Saved:** Listen to the cacophony of the saved animals as the Ark's door is finally sealed.
