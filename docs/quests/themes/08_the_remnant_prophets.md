# Overarching Theme VIII: The Remnant Prophets

**Core Concept:** Throughout the antediluvian age, a line of prophets maintained the truth against the overwhelming corruption of the Cainites and the Watchers. This questline has the player tracing the history of these patriarchs (from Enosh to Noah), recovering their lost teachings, and protecting their living descendants from assassination.

## Story Arc A: The Days of Enosh (The First Invocations)
*Location Span:* Edenic Basin -> Plains of Nod
1. **The First Prayer:** Discover the ancient stone circle where Enosh first taught men to call upon the name of the Lord.
2. **The Desecrated Circle:** Clear out a gang of Cainite thugs who are using the stone circle as a gambling den.
3. **The Lost Chants:** Recover 5 clay tablets containing the original hymns composed by Enosh.
4. **The Echoes of Worship:** Perform the ancient chants at the altar to temporarily cleanse the surrounding land of corruption.
5. **The Lineage Scroll:** Retrieve a stolen genealogical scroll detailing the descent from Seth to Enosh.
6. **The Heretic's Fire:** Stop a group of Watcher cultists from burning the remaining historical records of Enosh's time.
7. **The Hidden Shrine:** Locate a hidden shrine dedicated to Enosh in the deep plains.
8. **The Shrine's Guardian:** Defeat a corrupted earth elemental that has taken over the shrine.
9. **The Prophet's Tears:** Collect water from a spring said to have sprung from Enosh's tears over the world's decline.
10. **The Blessing of Enosh:** Drink from the spring to receive a permanent increase to maximum Mana.

## Story Arc B: The Legacy of Kenan and Mahalalel
*Location Span:* Plains of Nod -> City of Enoch
11. **The Healer's Path:** Follow the teachings of Mahalalel, the great healer, by curing 10 diseased nomads in Nod.
12. **The Blight of Cain:** Investigate a magical blight spreading from the City of Enoch into the plains.
13. **Mahalalel's Remedy:** Gather rare herbs from the edge of the Flaming Boundary to recreate the Patriarch's ultimate cure.
14. **The Infected Guards:** Use the remedy to cure city guards, winning their secret loyalty.
15. **The Wisdom of Kenan:** Find the hidden cave where Kenan recorded his visions of the future.
16. **The Cipher of Stones:** Solve a puzzle using engraved stones to unlock Kenan's inner sanctum.
17. **The Vision of Iron:** Read Kenan's prophecy regarding the rise of Azazel's metallurgy and the resulting bloodshed.
18. **The Preemptive Strike:** Sabotage a newly constructed iron forge in Enoch based on Kenan's warning.
19. **The Patriarch's Ring:** Recover Kenan's signet ring from a corrupt magistrate in the city.
20. **The Succession:** Deliver the ring to the current Sethite elder to legitimize his rule.

## Story Arc C: The Days of Jared (The Descent)
*Location Span:* Hermon Foothills
21. **The Naming of Jared:** Learn the history of Jared, whose name means "Descent," because in his days the Watchers fell.
22. **The Watcher's Landing:** Survey the massive crater in the foothills caused by the initial impact of the Grigori.
23. **The First Contact:** Recover a journal from a scout who witnessed the Watchers' arrival and survived.
24. **The Corrupted Soil:** Cleanse the earth around the impact crater using holy salts.
25. **Jared's Warning:** Deliver a warning originally penned by Jared to a stubborn Sethite chieftain who refuses to leave the mountain.
26. **The Chieftain's Folly:** Rescue the chieftain when his camp is inevitably overrun by Samyaza's cultists.
27. **The Patriarch's Shield:** Retrieve Jared's ancient wooden shield, which is surprisingly resistant to celestial magic.
28. **The Cult's Retaliation:** Defend the Sethite base camp from a coordinated attack by cultists seeking the shield.
29. **The Spirit of Jared:** Commune with the spirit of Jared at a high-altitude shrine to learn the Watchers' weaknesses.
30. **The Vow of Resistance:** Take a formal vow at the shrine to oppose the Grigori until the end of the age.

## Story Arc D: Enoch's Walk (The Seventh from Adam)
*Location Span:* City of Enoch -> Hermon Summit
31. **The Namesake City:** Secretly escort the patriarch Enoch through the city that shares his name, hiding him from Lamech's patrols.
32. **The Preaching of Doom:** Protect Enoch as he delivers a public prophecy of judgment in the central plaza.
33. **The Assassin's Blade:** Slay the elite Cainite assassins sent to silence the prophet.
34. **The Heavenly Tablets:** Accompany Enoch to the slopes of Hermon where he reads the heavenly tablets regarding the world's fate.
35. **The Transcriber:** Defend Enoch while he rapidly dictates the contents of the tablets to you.
36. **The Message to the Giants:** Deliver Enoch's written judgment to the Nephilim warlords (they will attack you upon reading it).
37. **The Intercessor:** Protect Enoch as he attempts to intercede with the Archangels on behalf of humanity.
38. **The Final Prophecy:** Record Enoch's final prophecy regarding the impending Flood.
39. **The Translation:** Witness the mystical event where Enoch "was not, for God took him."
40. **The Legacy:** Escape the mountain with Enoch's final writings before the Watchers can destroy them.

## Story Arc E: Methuselah and Lamech (The Final Wait)
*Location Span:* Plains of Nod -> Abyssal Basins
41. **The Longest Life:** Speak with Methuselah, the oldest man alive, who holds the collective memory of the entire age.
42. **The Burden of Memory:** Help Methuselah recover 5 sacred relics that he wishes to pass on to Noah before he dies.
43. **The Death of the Patriarch:** Defend Methuselah's deathbed from Nephilim scavengers seeking to consume his ancient power.
44. **The Mourning of the Earth:** Witness the earth itself reacting to Methuselah's passing (the lore states the Flood came the year he died).
45. **The Father of Rest:** Assist Noah's father, Lamech (the righteous one), in organizing the remaining Sethite families.
46. **The Clash of Lamechs:** Defend the righteous Lamech from an assassination attempt ordered by the evil King Lamech of Enoch.
47. **The Naming of Noah:** Find the ancient stone where Noah was named ("He will bring us rest").
48. **The Passing of the Torch:** Escort the final remnant of the Sethite elders to the Ark construction site.
49. **The Final Blessing:** Receive a blessing from the gathered elders, granting a permanent buff to all stats.
50. **The End of the Line:** As the rain begins, watch the last of the ancient patriarchs pass away peacefully, their duty complete.
