# Overarching Theme II: The Nephilim Scourge

**Core Concept:** The Nephilim are the unnatural offspring of the Watchers and human women. As they grew, their insatiable appetites led to the strip-mining of the earth, the devouring of all livestock, and eventually cannibalism. This theme tracks the player's efforts to protect humanity from the giants across the contiguous map.

## Story Arc A: The Famine (Ecological Collapse)
*Location Span:* Plains of Nod -> Nephilim Wastes
1. **The Barren Fields:** Investigate the failure of crops on the western edge of Nod.
2. **The Stolen Cattle:** Track a herd of Aurochs stolen by Nephilim scouts.
3. **The Slaughterhouse:** Discover the grim fate of the herd and recover whatever meat can be salvaged.
4. **The Crying Earth:** Collect 20 samples of soil that has been magically drained of nutrients by giant footfalls.
5. **The Dammed River:** Break the crude earthen dams the giants built to hoard the Tigris waters.
6. **The Thirsty Herds:** Escort a herd of gazelles to a newly freed watering hole.
7. **The Giant Locusts:** Slay the swarms of unnaturally large locusts that follow in the Nephilim's wake.
8. **The Druid's Cure:** Assist a Sethite druid in formulating a fertilizer to revive the dead earth.
9. **Planting the Seeds:** Defend the druid as they plant the restorative seeds in the heart of the Wastes.
10. **The Angry Earth:** Pacify the violent earth elementals that have been driven mad by the ecological destruction.

## Story Arc B: The Cannibal Turn (Horror Survival)
*Location Span:* Outer Enoch -> Nephilim Wastes -> Hermon Foothills
11. **The Missing Caravans:** Investigate a merchant caravan that disappeared on the road to Hermon.
12. **The Grisly Discovery:** Find the remains of the merchants in a Nephilim feasting pit.
13. **The Survivor's Tale:** Rescue a traumatized survivor who witnessed the giants turning to cannibalism.
14. **The Blood-Drinkers:** Hunt down 15 mutated, nocturnal Nephilim who actively hunt humans for blood.
15. **The Bone Totems:** Destroy 8 grotesque totems built from human remains to mark giant territory.
16. **The Bait:** Serve as bait to lure a massive Blood-Drinker out of its cave.
17. **The Rescue Mission:** Infiltrate a giant encampment to free 30 captured humans destined for the cooking pots.
18. **The Grieving Mother:** Recover the personal effects of 5 victims from the feasting pits.
19. **The Purifying Fire:** Burn the feasting pits to the ground to prevent disease from spreading.
20. **The Night Hunt:** Survive a 10-minute onslaught of starving Nephilim in the outer slums of Enoch.

## Story Arc C: The Warlords (Gilgamesh, Ohyah, and Hahyah)
*Location Span:* Nephilim Wastes -> City of Enoch
21. **The Epic of the Tyrant:** Find the first clay tablet detailing the exploits of Gilgamesh, the Nephilim king.
22. **The Tyrant's Guard:** Slay the elite brutes guarding the ruins of Gilgamesh's stronghold.
23. **The Stolen Crown:** Recover Gilgamesh's crown of star-metal to prove his mortality to his followers.
24. **The Dream of Ohyah:** Speak with Mahaway (a neutral giant) to learn of his brother Ohyah's prophetic nightmares.
25. **The Submerged Tablet:** Infiltrate Warlord Hahyah's camp to steal the tablet describing Ohyah's dream of the Flood.
26. **The Translation:** Bring the tablet to the patriarch Enoch for interpretation.
27. **The Warlord's Fury:** Defend Enoch's hiding place from Hahyah's assassins.
28. **The Clash of Warlords:** Manipulate the supply lines to force a territorial war between Ohyah's forces and Hahyah's forces.
29. **The Battlefield Scavenger:** Loot 15 pieces of heavy giant-armor from the battlefield while they fight.
30. **The Fall of Hahyah:** Assassinate Warlord Hahyah while he is weakened from the battle.

## Story Arc D: The Behemoth Riders
*Location Span:* Nephilim Wastes -> Abyssal Basins
31. **The Tremors:** Investigate rhythmic, massive tremors moving towards the southern basins.
32. **The Tamed Beasts:** Discover that the giants have begun domesticating prehistoric Behemoths.
33. **The Beast-Masters:** Slay 10 Giant Beast-Masters training the mounts.
34. **The Broken Yokes:** Destroy the massive iron yokes used to control the Behemoths.
35. **The Stampede:** Trigger a stampede of freed Behemoths through a Nephilim supply camp.
36. **The Rogue Behemoth:** Put down a Behemoth that has gone mad from the giants' abuse.
37. **The Calvary Charge:** Defend a Sethite settlement against a coordinated charge of Behemoth-riding giants.
38. **The Rider's Lance:** Steal the enchanted, 20-foot lance used by the lead rider.
39. **The Armor Plating:** Strip the crude armor plating off the Behemoths to make them vulnerable.
40. **The Grand Champion:** Defeat the Grand Champion of the Riders in single combat.

## Story Arc E: The Desperation (Pre-Flood)
*Location Span:* Abyssal Basins -> Ark Plateau
41. **The Realization:** Deliver the news to Mahaway that the Flood is inevitable and cannot be stopped.
42. **The Mad Rush:** Observe the giants panicking as the first drops of rain begin to fall.
43. **The Southern Migration:** Track the Nephilim horde as they march toward the high plateau of the Ark.
44. **The Chokepoint:** Hold the muddy pass leading to the plateau against 5 waves of desperate giants.
45. **The Siege Engines:** Destroy 4 massive trebuchets the giants brought to breach the plateau's defenses.
46. **The Climbing Horde:** Shoot down climbing giants attempting to scale the cliffs of the plateau.
47. **The Ark Ramp:** Stand shoulder-to-shoulder with Shem, Ham, and Japheth to defend the ramp leading into the Ark.
48. **The Drowning Brutes:** Slay giants who are violently thrashing in the rising waters, threatening the hull of the Ark.
49. **The Last Giant:** Defeat the final, massive Warlord as the water reaches your waists.
50. **The Silence of the Waters:** Watch from the deck as the last of the Nephilim sink beneath the waves.
