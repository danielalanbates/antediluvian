# Act V: Flood - Quest Database

The final days. The sky is storm-dark, rain falls endlessly, and the apocalypse has begun. The fountains of the great deep are breaking open to cleanse the earth of the Nephilim and the Watchers' corruption.

## Main Storyline Quests

### 1. Leviathan Hunt
*   **Quest Giver:** Noah (Near the Ark Construction Site)
*   **Lore:** "The fountains of the great deep burst forth, and with the rising waters come ancient terrors from the abyss. Leviathans are breaching the shallows, threatening our encampment. Hunt them down before the waters overtake us completely."
*   **Objective:** Hunt 3 Shallows Leviathans.
*   **Reward:** 800 XP, 90 Gold, Hide Vest.

### 2. The Fountains of the Deep
*   **Quest Giver:** Noah
*   **Lore:** "The earth's crust is shattering. Geysers of scalding abyssal water are erupting across the valley, blocking the migration path of the beasts we must save. Use these heavy boulders to temporarily cap the geysers."
*   **Objective:** Cap 5 Abyssal Geysers.
*   **Reward:** 900 XP, 100 Gold.

### 3. Boarding the Ark
*   **Quest Giver:** Shem
*   **Lore:** "The Nephilim have realized what we are doing. In their desperation, they are attacking the Ark to seize it for themselves. Hold the ramp! Let no giant set foot on the gopher wood!"
*   **Objective:** Defend the Ark Ramp against 3 waves of Desperate Nephilim.
*   **Reward:** 1200 XP, 150 Gold, Covenant Signet (Epic Ring).

## Side Quests

### 4. The Last Scion
*   **Quest Giver:** Japheth
*   **Lore:** "We received word that one righteous family remains trapped in a flooded outpost to the south. The waters are rising fast. Find them and escort them to the high ground before they drown."
*   **Objective:** Escort the Trapped Family to the Ark perimeter.
*   **Reward:** 850 XP, 90 Gold.

### 5. Drowning the Corruption
*   **Quest Giver:** Ham
*   **Lore:** "The Watchers left behind forbidden texts and scrolls of sorcery in their local temple. As the waters rise, the cultists are trying to save their dark library. Make sure those scrolls sink to the bottom of the new ocean."
*   **Objective:** Destroy 5 Crates of Forbidden Scrolls.
*   **Reward:** 800 XP, 85 Gold.

### 6. The Rain Begins (Survival)
*   **Quest Giver:** Noah
*   **Lore:** "The door is sealed. The skies have completely opened. The water is surging. Survive the final onslaught of drowning beasts, panicked giants, and abyssal horrors until the Ark breaks free of the earth!"
*   **Objective:** Survive for 5 minutes in the Rising Waters zone.
*   **Reward:** 1500 XP, 200 Gold, Title: "Survivor of the Deluge".
