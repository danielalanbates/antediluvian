# Act IV: Enoch - Quest Database

The First City. Built by Cain and named after his son, this is a massive, smog-choked metropolis representing the height of corrupted human civilization, built entirely on Watcher technology and warfare.

## Main Storyline Quests

### 1. Shades of Enoch
*   **Quest Giver:** City Dissident (Slums)
*   **Lore:** "This city is built on blood. The spirits of those murdered to lay its foundations still wander the smoggy streets, twisted by the sorcery practiced here. Put them to rest."
*   **Objective:** Banish 5 City Shades.
*   **Reward:** 500 XP, 55 Gold.

### 2. Azazel's Armory
*   **Quest Giver:** Lamech's Rival
*   **Lore:** "King Lamech boasts that if Cain is avenged sevenfold, he will be avenged seventy-sevenfold. He has stockpiled advanced Watcher armor in the central citadel. Break in and steal the schematics so we can find a weakness."
*   **Objective:** Steal 3 Watcher Armor Schematics from the Citadel.
*   **Reward:** 650 XP, 75 Gold, Lamech's Discarded Helm.

### 3. The Smog of Industry
*   **Quest Giver:** Asthmatic Refugee
*   **Lore:** "The great alchemical furnaces never stop burning. They refine metals and brew enchantments day and night. The ash is choking us. Destroy the furnace regulators in the industrial district to halt production."
*   **Objective:** Destroy 4 Alchemical Furnace Regulators.
*   **Reward:** 600 XP, 70 Gold.

## Side Quests

### 4. The Hidden Prophet
*   **Quest Giver:** Enoch the Patriarch (Hiding)
*   **Lore:** "I share a name with this accursed city, but not its fate. I have seen the heavenly tablets; judgment is decreed against the Watchers and this metropolis. Deliver this scroll of warning to the Mayor's compound."
*   **Objective:** Deliver the Scroll of Doom to the Mayor's Desk (requires stealth or brute force).
*   **Reward:** 550 XP, 60 Gold.

### 5. Song of the Sword
*   **Quest Giver:** Tubal-Cain's Descendant
*   **Lore:** "My forefather was the forger of all instruments of bronze and iron. But the weapons made today are infused with dark magic. Slay the Enchanter Smiths who are corrupting our ancestral craft."
*   **Objective:** Defeat 6 Enchanter Smiths.
*   **Reward:** 600 XP, 65 Gold, Enchanted Bronze Blade.

### 6. The Syndicate of Sorcery
*   **Quest Giver:** Dissident
*   **Lore:** "The followers of Samyaza have formed a syndicate that controls the city's underbelly. They use mind-altering root-cuttings to keep the slums docile. Break up their operation in the lower wards."
*   **Objective:** Slay 8 Syndicate Sorcerers and burn 3 Root Stashes.
*   **Reward:** 650 XP, 70 Gold.
