# The Mount Questline: "Taming the Antediluvian Wild"

In *Antediluvia*, mounts are not simply purchased from a vendor with gold. Because the world is wild and prehistoric, players must prove their worth and physically tame their first mount at **Level 40**. 

The standard mount for all classes is the **Dire-Wolf**, though Cainites and Sethites approach the taming process differently.

## Part 1: The Call of the Wild (Level 40)
*   **Quest Giver:** Beast-Master Jabal (Located at the border of the Plains of Nod and the City of Enoch)
*   **Lore:** "Your legs grow weary, traveler. The supercontinent is vast, and the Nephilim cover ground twice as fast as you. If you wish to survive the coming days, you must bond with a beast of the old world. But a Dire-Wolf bows only to an Alpha."
*   **Objective:** Slay 15 feral Dire-Wolves in the Plains of Nod to prove your dominance and collect their **Alpha Pheromones**.
*   **Reward:** 4,000 XP, Unlocks Part 2.

## Part 2: The Watcher’s Chain OR The Druid’s Bridle
*Depending on the player's alignment or choice, they must craft the item required to control the beast.*

**Option A (The Cainite Path - Domination):**
*   **Objective:** Travel to the Industrial Forges in the City of Enoch. Steal **3 Watcher-Forged Iron Links** from an Enchanter Smith and bring them to a corrupt armorer to forge the *Chain of Domination*.
*   **Lore:** "Azazel taught us to bend the earth to our will. With this iron, the beast will have no choice but to obey."

**Option B (The Sethite Path - Harmony):**
*   **Objective:** Travel to the Edenic Basin. Harvest **5 Vines of the First Grove** while avoiding the Cherubim's gaze, and weave them into the *Bridle of the Ancients*.
*   **Lore:** "We do not break the beasts; we partner with them as Adam did when he named them. This bridle signals respect, not subjugation."
*   **Reward (Both):** 5,500 XP, The Taming Item (Chain or Bridle), Unlocks Part 3.

## Part 3: The Alpha’s Den
*   **Quest Giver:** Beast-Master Jabal
*   **Lore:** "You have the scent of an Alpha, and you have the reins. Now you must find a mount worthy of them. Deep in the canyons of the Nephilim Wastes, a legendary Dire-Wolf pack makes its den. You must separate a young Alpha from the pack and subdue it."
*   **Objective:** Travel to the *Howling Canyons* in the Nephilim Wastes. Locate the elite mob **Swift-Claw (Level 40 Elite)**. You must fight Swift-Claw and bring its health below 15% without killing it. Once below 15%, use your Taming Item (Chain or Bridle) on the beast.
*   **Reward:** 7,000 XP, Unlocks Part 4.

## Part 4: The Ride Home
*   **Quest Giver:** Swift-Claw (Now an allied NPC)
*   **Lore:** The beast is subdued, but it must learn to carry a rider in the heat of battle. 
*   **Objective:** Ride Swift-Claw out of the canyon. You have 3 minutes to navigate a gauntlet of angry Nephilim Giants throwing boulders. If you are dismounted, you fail and must restart the gauntlet.
*   **Reward:** 8,500 XP, Skill: Riding (Apprentice).

## The Final Reward
Upon completing the gauntlet and returning to Beast-Master Jabal, he will officially bind the mount to you.
*   **Item Received:** *Horn of the Dire-Wolf* (Summons a mount that increases movement speed by 60%).
*   **Title Earned:** `<Name>, The Beast-Tamer`

*(Note: At Level 60, players will embark on a significantly harder, epic questline to obtain their 100% speed mount, which involves taming a prehistoric **Behemoth** or a **Giant Eagle**).*
