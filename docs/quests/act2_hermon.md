# Act II: Hermon - Quest Database

Mount Hermon is the rocky, mountainous, high-altitude zone where the 200 Watchers descended from heaven and bound themselves with an oath to corrupt humanity.

## Main Storyline Quests

### 1. Watchers on the Mount
*   **Quest Giver:** Jared the Patriarch (Base Camp)
*   **Lore:** "They fell like stars onto the summit. The Watchers... angels who abandoned their station. They are spreading forbidden knowledge to the tribes. We must thin their vanguard before their influence spreads."
*   **Objective:** Slay 5 Fallen Watchers.
*   **Reward:** 220 XP, 25 Gold.

### 2. The Oath of Imprecation
*   **Quest Giver:** Jared the Patriarch
*   **Lore:** "Samyaza and his followers bound themselves with a curse on the peak of this mountain. They carved their pact into a monolithic stone. Find the Oath-Stone and shatter it to break their spiritual hold over the foothills."
*   **Objective:** Destroy the Oath-Stone at the summit of Hermon.
*   **Reward:** 350 XP, 40 Gold, Amulet of the Unbound.

### 3. Stargazer's Fall
*   **Quest Giver:** Enoch the Prophet (Wandering)
*   **Lore:** "Baraqiel taught men to read the stars, not for navigation, but for divination and twisted prophecies. His chief disciple, the Stargazer, is rallying cultists on the eastern ridge. Silence him."
*   **Objective:** Defeat the Chief Stargazer (Elite).
*   **Reward:** 450 XP, 50 Gold, Astrologer's Staff.

## Side Quests

### 4. Roots of Sorcery
*   **Quest Giver:** Healer Mahalalel (Base Camp)
*   **Lore:** "Samyaza taught the women how to cut roots and brew enchantments. These corrupted herbs are poisoning the mountain springs. Gather the toxic Mandrake Roots so I can burn them safely."
*   **Objective:** Collect 8 Toxic Mandrake Roots.
*   **Reward:** 200 XP, 20 Gold, 2x Minor Healing Potion.

### 5. The Cosmetic Deception
*   **Quest Giver:** Sethite Scout
*   **Lore:** "Azazel taught them the making of bracelets, ornaments, and the beautifying of eyelids to seduce and deceive. A caravan carrying these cursed vanity items is passing through the mountain pass. Intercept it."
*   **Objective:** Destroy 3 Caravan Wagons of Azazel.
*   **Reward:** 250 XP, 35 Gold.

### 6. Visions of the Gateway
*   **Quest Giver:** Enoch the Prophet
*   **Lore:** "The base of this mountain hides deep caverns... some say they are the very gates of the underworld, cracked open by the weight of the Watchers' sin. Slay the cave-dwellers that have been twisted by the abyssal energies."
*   **Objective:** Defeat 10 Chasm Fiends in the Grotto of Pan.
*   **Reward:** 300 XP, 40 Gold.
