# Act III: Nephilim - Quest Database

A dusty, red wasteland. The offspring of the Watchers—the Nephilim—have multiplied. Their immense size and insatiable appetites have stripped the land bare, leading to widespread violence and cannibalism.

## Main Storyline Quests

### 1. Giants in the Land
*   **Quest Giver:** Desperate Refugee (Canyon Entrance)
*   **Lore:** "There are giants in the land. They ate all our crops, our cattle, and now... they are looking at us. We cannot outrun them. You must bring down their hunting party so we can escape."
*   **Objective:** Slay 5 Nephilim Brutes.
*   **Reward:** 350 XP, 40 Gold.

### 2. Blood and Iron
*   **Quest Giver:** Renegade Armorer
*   **Lore:** "The giants don't just use clubs; they wield massive iron swords forged using Azazel's teachings. Their armory is in the deep ravine. Sabotage their weapon caches before they march on the remaining human settlements."
*   **Objective:** Sabotage 4 Nephilim Weapon Caches.
*   **Reward:** 400 XP, 45 Gold, Iron-Plated Greaves.

### 3. Ohyah's Dream
*   **Quest Giver:** Mahaway (A Neutral Giant)
*   **Lore:** "My brother Ohyah had a terrifying dream... a great tablet submerged in water, its letters washed away. We fear judgment is coming. Retrieve the tablets of Ohyah's dream from the rival warlord Hahyah, so I may take them to Enoch for interpretation."
*   **Objective:** Retrieve the Dream Tablets from Warlord Hahyah (Boss).
*   **Reward:** 600 XP, 70 Gold, Ring of the Dreamer.

## Side Quests

### 4. The Ravaged Earth
*   **Quest Giver:** Druid of the Old Way
*   **Lore:** "The giants have consumed everything. The earth itself is dying beneath their feet. Bring me samples of the depleted soil from their feeding grounds so I can attempt to synthesize a restorative blessing."
*   **Objective:** Collect 5 Depleted Soil Samples.
*   **Reward:** 300 XP, 30 Gold.

### 5. Bones of the Consumed
*   **Quest Giver:** Grieving Mother
*   **Lore:** "When the food ran out, they turned on humanity. My family was taken to their feasting pits. Please, recover whatever remains you can find so I can give them a proper burial."
*   **Objective:** Recover 6 Human Remains from the Feasting Pits.
*   **Reward:** 350 XP, 35 Gold.

### 6. The Blood-Drinkers
*   **Quest Giver:** Refugee Guard
*   **Lore:** "Some of the giants have developed a taste for blood, mutating into something even more horrific. They hunt at night in the canyons. Clear out the canyon so our scouts can pass."
*   **Objective:** Slay 8 Blood-Drinker Nephilim.
*   **Reward:** 450 XP, 50 Gold, Giant-Bone Crusher (Mace).
