# Act I: Eden - Quest Database

The zone of Eden represents the early days of humanity outside the Garden. The land is lush but increasingly hostile, bearing the curse of thorns and the legacy of Cain's early strife.

## Main Storyline Quests

### 1. Serpents in the Garden
*   **Quest Giver:** Elder Adamah (Zone Entry)
*   **Lore:** "The subtle beasts that once whispered lies now breed in the tall grass. They defile the memory of the Garden. Slay them before their venom spreads further."
*   **Objective:** Slay 5 Serpents.
*   **Reward:** 120 XP, 15 Gold, Bronze Sword.

### 2. The Altar of the Firstborn
*   **Quest Giver:** Sethite Wanderer (Crossroads)
*   **Lore:** "My ancestor Abel built an altar of unhewn stone here, long before the earth drank his blood. The Cainite scavengers are trying to dismantle it. Drive them away."
*   **Objective:** Defeat 8 Cainite Scavengers near the Ruined Altar.
*   **Reward:** 180 XP, 20 Gold.

### 3. The Flaming Sword
*   **Quest Giver:** Elder Adamah
*   **Lore:** "To the east lies the true Garden, forever barred by the Cherubim and the turning sword of fire. But strange elemental sparks have drifted across the boundary, igniting the brush. Extinguish the stray embers."
*   **Objective:** Collect 5 Cherubim Sparks from the eastern border.
*   **Reward:** 250 XP, 30 Gold, Spark-Woven Cloak.

## Side Quests

### 4. Fruit of the Thorns
*   **Quest Giver:** Evie (Gatherer's Camp)
*   **Lore:** "The ground is cursed to yield thorns and thistles, but we must still eat. The briar-fruit is bitter, but nourishing. Gather some from the thicket, but beware the wild boars."
*   **Objective:** Collect 10 Briar-Fruit.
*   **Reward:** 100 XP, 5 Gold, 3x Bitter Bread (Consumable).

### 5. Blood on the Soil
*   **Quest Giver:** Abel's Echo (Spirit at the Altar)
*   **Lore:** "The voice of my blood cries out from the ground. The earth itself is restless here. Calm the agitated earth elementals that rise from the stained soil."
*   **Objective:** Defeat 5 Restless Earth Elementals.
*   **Reward:** 200 XP, 25 Gold.

### 6. The First Forges
*   **Quest Giver:** Tubal-Cain's Apprentice (Defector)
*   **Lore:** "My master has begun striking metal from the earth, teaching men the arts of war. This knowledge is dangerous. Steal the early bronze ingots from their camp so they cannot forge more blades."
*   **Objective:** Loot 6 Bronze Ingots from the Cainite Camp.
*   **Reward:** 220 XP, 35 Gold, Bronze Bracers.
