# Location 02: The Metropolitan Sprawl of Enoch

## Seamless Geography
Walking west from the Plains of Nod, the horizon gradually darkens with smog. The transition is seamless: the hard dirt gives way to primitive cobblestones, then massive megalithic structures. This is the **City of Enoch**, built by Cain and expanded by his lineage over centuries. 
It is a massive, continent-spanning urban blight. It consists of the **Outer Slums**, the **Industrial Forges** (where Azazel's metallurgy is practiced), and the **Central Ziggurat of Lamech**. 
To the northwest, the city's walls give way to the rocky ascent of **The Hermon Range** (Location 03). To the south and west, the city bleeds into the strip-mined **Nephilim Wastes** (Location 04).

## Quest Chains (The Corruption of Civilization)

### The Outer Slums
1. **The Ash-Choked Streets:** (Giver: Slum Doctor) Deliver primitive respirators (crafted from filtered moss) to 10 dying civilians in the outer ring.
2. **The Bread Riots:** (Giver: Slum Doctor) Quell a riot started by starving citizens who have been denied grain by the inner city guards.
3. **Syndicate Thugs:** (Giver: Black-Market Merchant) Defeat 12 Syndicate Enforcers extorting the outer markets.
4. **The Root-Cutting Trade:** (Giver: Black-Market Merchant) Confiscate crates of mind-altering mandrake roots imported from Hermon.
5. **The Missing Children:** (Giver: Desperate Mother) Track down a group of orphans who were abducted to work in the inner city forges.
6. **Shadows in the Alley:** (Giver: Night-Watchman) Hunt down the Shade-Hounds that prowl the slums at midnight.
7. **The Resistance Underground:** (Giver: Night-Watchman) Find the hidden entrance to the dissident safehouse beneath the slums.

### The Industrial Forges (Azazel's Legacy)
8. **The Fires of Industry:** (Giver: Resistance Leader) Sabotage 5 primary bellows powering the great alchemical furnaces.
9. **Iron and Blood:** (Giver: Resistance Leader) Slay 8 Enchanter Smiths who are folding dark magic into the city's weaponry.
10. **The Prototype Armor:** (Giver: Defecting Smith) Steal the blueprints for a new, impenetrable suit of Watcher-plate armor.
11. **The Furnace Golem:** (Giver: Defecting Smith) Destroy the animated slag-golem guarding the central foundry.
12. **Toxic Runoff:** (Giver: Saboteur) Plug the drainage pipes that are pumping toxic magical runoff into the Gihon River.
13. **The Overseer's Whip:** (Giver: Saboteur) Assassinate the brutal Forge Overseer and take his enchanted whip.
14. **Liberating the Forges:** (Giver: Resistance Leader) Escort the freed orphan laborers out of the industrial district and into the slums.

### The Central Ziggurat (Lamech's Domain)
15. **The Warlord's Boast:** (Giver: Enoch the Patriarch) Lamech claims seventy-sevenfold vengeance. Infiltrate the lower tiers of his Ziggurat and discover the source of his power.
16. **The Harem Guards:** (Giver: Enoch the Patriarch) Defeat Lamech's elite royal guard, who wield Watcher-forged halberds.
17. **The Blood Oaths:** (Giver: Spy) Recover the ledger of blood-oaths sworn by the city's nobles to the Watchers of Hermon.
18. **The Sorcerer's Tower:** (Giver: Spy) Climb the eastern spire and defeat the High Astrologer tracking the heavenly movements.
19. **Disabling the Ward:** (Giver: Resistance Mage) Destroy the four cosmic anchors shielding the Ziggurat's throne room from magical attack.
20. **Lamech's Champion:** (Giver: Resistance Leader) Defeat the hulking, half-giant champion that guards Lamech's inner sanctum.
21. **The Stolen Crown:** (Giver: Resistance Leader) Escape the Ziggurat with Lamech's crown of iron, proving to the city that he is not invincible.

### The City Underbelly (The Sewers & Catacombs)
22. **The City of the Dead:** (Giver: Gravedigger) Enter the catacombs beneath Enoch and clear out the restless Shades of murdered laborers.
23. **The Necromancer of Nod:** (Giver: Gravedigger) Stop a dark mage who is attempting to bind the Shades into an undead army.
24. **The Sunken Vault:** (Giver: Treasure Hunter) Solve the puzzle of the ancient water-locks to access a vault of pre-corruption relics.
25. **The Rat-King:** (Giver: Treasure Hunter) Cull the massive, mutated rodents breeding in the magical runoff of the sewers.
26. **The Secret Passage:** (Giver: Resistance Leader) Map the subterranean tunnel that leads beyond the city walls, directly toward the foothills of Mount Hermon.

### The Western Gate (Transition to the Wastes)
27. **The Giant Toll:** (Giver: Gate Captain) Nephilim from the wastes are demanding a toll of cattle. Slay their emissary to send a message.
28. **Reinforcing the Barricade:** (Giver: Gate Captain) Collect 20 Iron Beams from the forges to reinforce the western gate against giant attacks.
29. **The Deserters:** (Giver: Gate Captain) Hunt down city guards who abandoned their posts out of fear of the Nephilim.
30. **Into the Wastes:** (Giver: Enoch the Patriarch) Step through the western gate and into the red desert to seek out the source of the giant infestation.
