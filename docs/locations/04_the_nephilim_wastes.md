# Location 04: The Nephilim Wastes

## Seamless Geography
Bordering the western edge of the City of Enoch and the southern slopes of Mount Hermon is a massive, contiguous red desert known as the **Nephilim Wastes**. 
Originally, this land was a fertile savanna, but the insatiable appetites of the Nephilim (the giant offspring of the Watchers) have stripped it bare. 
The transition is marked by the abrupt end of vegetation and the beginning of immense, strip-mined canyons, massive feeding pits, and crude iron fortifications. Walking west and south across the Wastes eventually leads a traveler downward in elevation into the **Abyssal Basins** (Location 05).

## Quest Chains (The Hunger of the Giants)

### The Strip Mines (Resource Depletion)
1. **The Ruined Earth:** (Giver: Druid of the Old Way) Collect 10 samples of the magically-depleted red soil to prove the land is dying.
2. **Iron for the Giants:** (Giver: Druid of the Old Way) Sabotage the massive iron quarries where enslaved humans are mining ore for the Nephilim armories.
3. **The Slave Drivers:** (Giver: Enslaved Miner) Slay 8 Giant Overseers who are working the human slaves to death in the pits.
4. **Breaking the Chains:** (Giver: Enslaved Miner) Locate the master forge and steal the heavy iron keys to unlock the slave collars.
5. **The Stolen Grain:** (Giver: Refugee) Recover 5 massive silos of grain hoarded by the giants before it rots.
6. **The Escort of the Broken:** (Giver: Refugee) Escort a train of freed, starving miners across the open wastes back to the safety of the city outskirts.

### The Feasting Pits (The Turn to Cannibalism)
7. **Bones of the Consumed:** (Giver: Grieving Mother) The giants have eaten all the cattle and have turned on humanity. Search the grisly feasting pits for human remains for proper burial.
8. **The Blood-Drinkers:** (Giver: Watcher-Hunter) Hunt down the mutated, nocturnal Nephilim who have developed a taste for drinking blood directly from their victims.
9. **The Charred Totems:** (Giver: Watcher-Hunter) Burn down the grotesque bone-totems the giants use to mark their hunting territories.
10. **The Matriarch's Lair:** (Giver: Watcher-Hunter) Infiltrate the deep canyon caves to slay a grotesque Nephilim Matriarch breeding more monstrosities.
11. **Purging the Trough:** (Giver: Priest) Pour purifying salt into the massive communal blood-troughs to sicken the feeding giants.

### The Warlords' Camps (The Fragments of Gilgamesh & Ohyah)
12. **The Warlord's Tribute:** (Giver: Mahaway the Neutral Giant) Slay 10 Nephilim Brutes to disrupt the tribute lines feeding the Warlord Hahyah.
13. **Ohyah's Dream:** (Giver: Mahaway) Warlord Ohyah had a prophetic dream of a massive flood. Infiltrate his camp and steal the clay tablets detailing his terrifying vision.
14. **The Epic of the First Tyrant:** (Giver: Scholar) Gather 4 fragments of the Epic of Gilgamesh, a legendary Nephilim king, from the ruins of his former command tent.
15. **The Clash of Titans:** (Giver: Mahaway) Lure two rival giant chieftains into the same canyon so they destroy each other in a territorial dispute.
16. **The Stolen Weapons:** (Giver: Resistance Fighter) The giants wield massive, crude iron swords forged by Azazel's teachings. Sabotage 3 of their primary weapon racks.
17. **The Behemoth Riders:** (Giver: Scout) Some giants have begun taming prehistoric Behemoths as mounts. Slay the Beast-Master giant and scatter his mounts.

### The Desolate Borders (Approaching the Floodplains)
18. **The Fleeing Herds:** (Giver: Scout) Guide a stampede of wild Aurochs away from the giant hunting grounds and towards the southern basins.
19. **The Drying Springs:** (Giver: Water Priest) The giants have dammed the last remaining freshwater springs. Destroy the crude stone dams to let the water flow south.
20. **The Scent of Rain:** (Giver: Noah's Emissary) You meet a messenger from the far south claiming a great storm is coming. Escort him safely through the Wastes so he can deliver his warning to the City of Enoch.
21. **The Pre-Flood Tremors:** (Giver: Noah's Emissary) Place 3 seismic measuring stakes in the hardened ground to track the deep tectonic shifts preceding the Deluge.
