# Location 03: The Hermon Range

## Seamless Geography
North-West of the smoggy sprawl of Enoch, the terrain begins a steep, massive ascent. The cobblestone streets of the city fade into treacherous, winding mountain passes. This is the **Hermon Range**. 
There are no loading boundaries; the elevation simply climbs continuously. It is divided into three contiguous biomes: the **Foothills & Caverns** (where Watcher cults have established camps), the **Alpine Ascent** (where the air thins and the celestial influence becomes visible), and the **Summit of the Oath** (the freezing peak where the 200 Watchers descended). 
Looking down from the summit, you can see the entire City of Enoch to the southeast and the red Nephilim Wastes to the south.

## Quest Chains (The Ascent of the Fallen)

### The Foothills & Cultist Camps
1. **The Mountain Pass:** (Giver: Sethite Scout) Escort a caravan of supplies up the first steep incline, defending against mountain lions.
2. **The Cult of Samyaza:** (Giver: Sethite Scout) Infiltrate the lower cultist camps and steal their manifesto of forbidden teachings.
3. **Roots of the Mountain:** (Giver: Alchemist) Harvest the rare, glow-in-the-dark mountain roots that have been mutated by celestial energy.
4. **The Enthralling Smoke:** (Giver: Alchemist) Destroy the braziers burning hallucinogenic herbs that keep the cultists docile.
5. **The Gateway of Pan:** (Giver: Oracle) Explore the deep cave at the base of the mountain and defeat the Chasm Fiends guarding the entrance to the deep earth.
6. **Sacrifices of the Foolish:** (Giver: Escaped Prisoner) Free 10 prisoners from cages before they are sacrificed in a dark ritual to the Watchers.
7. **The Cult High Priest:** (Giver: Escaped Prisoner) Assassinate the High Priest preaching the gospel of Azazel at the Foothill Altar.

### The Alpine Ascent (The Thinning Air)
8. **The Freezing Winds:** (Giver: Frostbitten Climber) Gather 15 thick pelts from Alpine Wolves to craft cold-weather gear required for the higher ascent.
9. **The Stargazer's Observatory:** (Giver: Frostbitten Climber) Assault an observatory built into the cliffside where disciples of Baraqiel track the stars.
10. **Shattering the Lenses:** (Giver: Frostbitten Climber) Smash the giant, polished quartz lenses the astrologers use for their divinations.
11. **The Celestial Elementals:** (Giver: Mystic Wanderer) Defeat the Star-Sparks—elemental beings of pure starlight bound by the Watchers to patrol the ascent.
12. **The Frozen Chasm:** (Giver: Mystic Wanderer) Navigate a perilous ice bridge while defending against diving Frost-Drakes.
13. **The Watcher's Vanguard:** (Giver: Mystic Wanderer) Slay 8 Lesser Grigori (angelic soldiers) stationed at the midpoint of the mountain.
14. **The Whispering Ice:** (Giver: Scholar) Chip away the ancient ice to reveal and transcribe the runic languages taught by Penemue.

### The Summit of the Oath (The Descent Point)
15. **The Final Ascent:** (Giver: Jared the Patriarch) Survive the blistering blizzard and push to the peak where the 200 descended.
16. **The First Defectors:** (Giver: Jared the Patriarch) Slay 5 high-ranking Fallen Watchers guarding the summit plateau.
17. **The Stone of Imprecation:** (Giver: Jared the Patriarch) Destroy the massive, cursed monolith where the Watchers swore their mutual oath of rebellion.
18. **The Blood of the Fallen:** (Giver: Alchemist) Collect vials of celestial ichor dropped by the Watchers to research their weaknesses.
19. **The Altar of Defiance:** (Giver: Enoch the Prophet) Cleanse the central altar with holy oil to disrupt the magical beacon summoning more celestial rebels.
20. **Commander of the 200:** (Giver: Enoch the Prophet) Defeat a named Captain of tens (a massive Watcher boss) attempting to reconstruct the Oath-Stone.
21. **The View from the Top:** (Giver: Cartographer) Plant a signaling beacon at the absolute highest peak to warn the valleys below of the impending threat.

### The Descent into the Wastes
22. **The Southern Slope:** (Giver: Cartographer) Chart the treacherous, rocky path leading down the southern face of Hermon toward the Nephilim Wastes.
23. **The Giant's Footprints:** (Giver: Scout) Measure and track the massive, petrified footprints left by the first generation of giants descending the mountain.
24. **Avalanche:** (Giver: Scout) Trigger a controlled avalanche to bury a camp of Nephilim reinforcements ascending the slope.
25. **The Stolen Supply Crate:** (Giver: Injured Courier) Recover a crate of vital rations stolen by a wandering Giant Eagle.
26. **The Line of Snow:** (Giver: Scout) Travel from the snowline down into the arid, red dust of the Wastes without resting, surviving the extreme temperature shock.
