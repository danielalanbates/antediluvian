# Location 01: The Edenic Basin & The Plains of Nod

## Seamless Geography
This is the easternmost contiguous region of the supercontinent. There are no loading screens. To the far east lies the impassable Flaming Boundary of the Garden itself. The region slopes downward into the **Edenic Basin**, a lush, green-gold riverland where the four primeval rivers (Pishon, Gihon, Tigris, Euphrates) converge. 
As you walk physically westward, the lush grass seamlessly thins out, the soil becomes harder, and you enter the **Plains of Nod** (The Land of Wandering). The Plains of Nod stretch out endlessly toward the horizon, eventually blending directly into the massive urban sprawl of Enoch (Location 02). 

## Quest Chains (The Legacy of the Firstborn)

### The River Pishon (Gathering & Early Wildlife)
1. **The Gold of Havilah:** (Giver: Sethite Elder) Dredge the river Pishon for primeval gold dust to craft your first sacred relic.
2. **Onyx and Bdellium:** (Giver: Sethite Elder) Mine onyx stones from the riverbanks before the Cainite scavengers steal them.
3. **The Serpent's Brood:** (Giver: Scout Abel) Cull the overpopulating giant serpents near the river mouth.
4. **Venom of the Ancient:** (Giver: Scout Abel) Extract venom glands from the serpent matriarch for antidote research.
5. **The Cursed Thistle:** (Giver: Gatherer Eve) Clear the encroaching, unnaturally fast-growing thorn bushes suffocating the grain.
6. **Taming the Aurochs:** (Giver: Herdsman) Subdue and rope wild Aurochs that have been spooked by distant Watcher magic.
7. **The Lost Shepherd:** (Giver: Herdsman) Find a missing shepherd boy who wandered too close to the Flaming Boundary.

### The Flaming Boundary (The Forbidden East)
8. **Sparks of the Cherubim:** (Giver: Mystic) Collect elemental embers that fly off the Cherubim's turning sword to light the camp's eternal flame.
9. **The Charred Glade:** (Giver: Mystic) Investigate a section of forest that was instantly petrified by a Cherub's gaze.
10. **Whispers Through the Fire:** (Giver: Mystic) Stand near the boundary and record the angelic hymnals, surviving the sheer sonic damage they emit.
11. **The Ash-Walkers:** (Giver: Guard Captain) Defeat ash-infused elementals that spawn from the scorched earth near the gate.

### The Crossroads of Strife (Entering Nod)
12. **The First Altar:** (Giver: Spirit of Abel) Rebuild the stones of the first altar that was kicked over by Cain's descendants.
13. **Blood on the Soil:** (Giver: Spirit of Abel) Defeat the restless earth-spirits that have been agitated by the first murder.
14. **The Mark of the Wanderer:** (Giver: Exiled Cainite) Escort a marked exile safely through the transition zone into Nod.
15. **Scavengers of Nod:** (Giver: Caravan Guard) Clear a camp of outlaws preying on travelers moving between the Basin and the Plains.
16. **The Vulture's Feast:** (Giver: Caravan Guard) Burn the rotting carcasses left by the scavengers to stop disease.

### The Deep Plains of Nod
17. **The Endless Horizon:** (Giver: Nomad Leader) Map the three watering holes across the arid expanse of Nod.
18. **Dust Devils:** (Giver: Nomad Leader) Disrupt the magical dust-storms conjured by rogue Watcher-cultists hiding in the plains.
19. **The Nomad's Yoke:** (Giver: Blacksmith) Recover stolen bronze tools from a raiding party of early Nephilim runts.
20. **Echoes of the City:** (Giver: Scout) As you approach the western edge of Nod, scout the massive, smoke-belching walls of Enoch in the distance.
21. **The Iron Caravan:** (Giver: Scout) Intercept a caravan transporting Azazel's iron from the City of Enoch into the plains.
22. **The Runaway Smith:** (Giver: Tubal-Cain's Apprentice) Protect a defector from Enoch who is being hunted by Lamech's assassins across the plains.
23. **The First Warhounds:** (Giver: Tubal-Cain's Apprentice) Slay the armored dire-wolves unleashed by the assassins.
24. **A Message to the Righteous:** (Giver: Dying Messenger) Deliver a warning about the Watchers' descent to the Sethite encampment back in the Basin.

### The Gihon River Delta
25. **The Murky Waters:** (Giver: Fisherman) Investigate the sudden pollution flowing down the Gihon river from the city of Enoch.
26. **The Sludge Crawlers:** (Giver: Fisherman) Eradicate the mutated amphibians spawning in the polluted delta.
27. **Purifying the Source:** (Giver: Water Priest) Place purifying lapis lazuli stones at the river's three main tributaries.
28. **The River Guardian:** (Giver: Water Priest) Awaken and defeat the ancient River Guardian to prove your strength.
29. **The Stolen Nets:** (Giver: Fisherman) Recover specialized fishing nets stolen by Nod outlaws.
30. **Feast of the Patriarchs:** (Giver: Village Elder) Gather 100 portions of clean meat to supply the great gathering before the migration west.
