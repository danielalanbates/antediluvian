# Antediluvia: Seamless World Locations & Quests

This directory contains the master geographical and quest layouts for the contiguous supercontinent of Antediluvia. 

## Design Philosophy: No Loading Screens
Unlike games with separate, instanced "zones" (like FFXI), Antediluvia is designed as a single, contiguous landmass. The transition from the lush eastern riverlands to the smoggy metropolitan sprawl, up the freezing alpine mountains, and down into the flooded basins is seamless. 

The quests designed here reflect this open-world structure. They frequently require players to cross regional borders (e.g., intercepting iron caravans moving from Enoch into Nod, or tracking giant footprints from the slopes of Hermon down into the Nephilim Wastes).

## The Contiguous Map (East to West)

1. [01: The Edenic Basin & The Plains of Nod](01_eden_and_nod.md) - The lush eastern origin point, bordering the flaming sword of the Cherubim, transitioning seamlessly into the hard dirt of Nod.
2. [02: The Metropolitan Sprawl of Enoch](02_the_sprawl_of_enoch.md) - The massive, continent-spanning first city built by Cain, filled with Watcher-tech industrial forges and slums.
3. [03: The Hermon Range](03_the_hermon_range.md) - The colossal mountain range northwest of Enoch, where the 200 Watchers descended to swear their oath.
4. [04: The Nephilim Wastes](04_the_nephilim_wastes.md) - The red, strip-mined desert south of Hermon and west of Enoch, stripped bare by the insatiable giant offspring.
5. [05: The Abyssal Basins (Floodplains)](05_the_abyssal_basins.md) - The lowest elevations on the continent where the Ark rests on a plateau, as the tectonic fountains of the deep break open to drown the world.

## Quest Volume
Across these 5 interconnected regions, there are **116** deeply lore-integrated quests (both main storylines and side quests) detailing the fall of the antediluvian age.
